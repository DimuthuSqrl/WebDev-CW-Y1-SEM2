Overwatch

Philadelphia Fusion continues to be the fan-favorite and one of the most influential organizations in the Overwatch League. Their Academy team Fusion University dominate the NA Contenders scene so much they decided to try their luck in Korea and eventually partnered and rebranded to T1. Over the past three years, Fusion has gone from Season 1 Championship finalists to the middle of the pack to being a dominating team again in 2020. Regardless of performance, their fans are loyally cheering them on their path to claiming their first OWL Championship.

Cricket

A thrusting Jamaican left-hander, Gayle earned himself a black mark on his first senior tour - to England in 2000 - where the new boys were felt to be insufficiently respectful of their elders. But a lack of respect, for opposition bowlers at least, has served Gayle well since then. Tall and imposing at the crease, he loves to carve through the covers off either foot, and has the ability to decimate the figures of even the thriftiest of opening bowlers. And in this era of Twenty20 cricket, Gayle is the batsman who has thrived like no other.
